T's Ramen



A Brief Word About Magic

My partner Taylor, to reveal her a full government name, is quite possibly a wizard. I've had my suspicions for a while, borne of her love of cats, affection for the moon and vague witchyness that surrounds her, but it wasn't until I tried these noodles that I realised she really could do magic.

So, from our cauldron to yours, here's T's Magic Ramen.

Ingredients

  • Shin Ramyun Noodles - 1 Pack
  • Roast Chicken Slices - 2 Pieces
  • Egg - 1
  • Mozarella - To Taste
  • Spring Onions - 1
  • Edamame beans - As many as you like
  • Water - 1 Jug
  • Optional: Microwaveable bao buns

    • Method

    1. Add 500ml
    2. Add soup and vegetable sachets from the ramen pack
    3. Bring the water to the boil
    4. Add edamame beans (Wizard note: buy the frozen ones, fresh ones go funky)
    5. Add chicken (Wizard note: swat your boyfriend's hand away & pay the chicken tax to your familiars)
    6. Say the magic words "cut up some spring onions, boyfriend."
    7. Add the ramen to the broth
    8. Crack an egg into the broth and cook for as long as this song lasts
    9. Stir around the edge of the pan without disturbing the egg
    10. Transfer the broth from pan to bowl
    11. Sprinkle the mozarella on & pay the cheese tax to your familiars
    12. Sprinkle the spring onion on
    13. Optional: Microwave the bao buns and add to the broth
