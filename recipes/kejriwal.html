Most of the best recipes are stolen anyway.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread - 2 Slices
  • Cheddar cheese - As much as you like
  • Eggs - 2
  • Green chillis- 1
  • Spring Onions - 1
  • Ground pepper
  • Heinz baked beans - 1 tin
  • Garam Masala - As much as you like
  • Ketchup

    • Method

    1. Chop the spring onions and put them in a bowl
    2. Chop the chilli and add it to the bowl
    3. Grate the cheddar and add it to the bowl
    4. Smush the spring onions, chilli, and cheese together
    5. Spread the contents of the bowl onto the bread
    6. Put the bread on the grill (author's note: you can preheat the grill or not, it's fine)
    7. Heat the beans on the pan and stir in the garam masala,
    8. Add the bread to the plate and pour the beans over
    9. Cover the bread and the beans in a ten-second's count worth of ketchup. Repeat.
